RELATIONAL SCHEMA

  • Users (user_id, username, email, password, role, name, semester, department)
  • Subjects (subject_id, title, description, code, teacher_id)
    Foreign Key: teacher_id → Users(user_id)
  • Courses (course_id, title, description, code, semester, department)
  • Course_Subjects (cs_id, course_id, subject_id)
    Foreign Key: course_id → Courses(course_id)
    Foreign Key: subject_id → Subjects(subject_id)
  • Enrollments (enrollment_id, student_id, course_id, enrolled_at)
    Foreign Key: student_id → Users(user_id)
    Foreign Key: course_id → Courses(course_id)
  • Feedback_Forms (form_id, course_id, title, description, is_active)
    Foreign Key: course_id → Courses(course_id)
  • Feedback_Form_Questions (question_id, form_id, question_text, question_type, question_order)
    Foreign Key: form_id → Feedback_Forms(form_id)
  • Feedback_Responses (response_id, student_id, form_id, subject_id, question_id, response_value, rating)
    Foreign Key: student_id → Users(user_id)
    Foreign Key: form_id → Feedback_Forms(form_id)
    Foreign Key: subject_id → Subjects(subject_id)
    Foreign Key: question_id → Feedback_Form_Questions(question_id)
